    About Me

    Hi, my name is Oladapo and I work as a Data Analyst. I hold a Bachelor's and Master's Degree in Business Management and Entrepreneurship respectively. I also hold a Post Graduate Certificate in Data Science and Business Analytics from The University of Texas at Austin where I finished in the top 10% of my cohort.
    From a young age, I have always enjoyed solving puzzles, whether it was a crossword , Sudoku or a rubiks cube, I spent a lot of time trying to crack it. Data Analysis to me provides challenges/puzzles that need to be solved. I very much enjoy the deductive reasoning and analytical thinking that goes into it and i'm excited everyday knowing that I get to do this for a living.
    Before transitioning fully into the dataverse, I had gained experience working in Strategy for over 4 years. This experience has allowed me understand problems not just from a technical perspective alone but also from the business perspective which has inherently made me a better data analyst as i'm able to align the technical requirements of a project with business requirements thereby creating a solution that is valuable to end users.
